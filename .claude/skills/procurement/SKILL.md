### 3. Cost Optimization Strategies

#### Per-Call vs. Contract
| Approach | When to Use | Pros | Cons |
|----------|-------------|------|------|
| **Per-Call** | Irregular needs, specialty work | Flexibility, no commitment | Higher cost, unreliable |
| **Contract** | Regular needs, predictable volume | Lower cost, reliability | Commitment, less flexibility |

#### Cost Reduction Tactics
1. **Bulk purchasing**: Combine needs across estates
2. **Long-term contracts**: Negotiate better rates
3. **Competitive bidding**: Get 3+ quotes always
4. **Payment terms**: Negotiate 30-60 day payment
5. **Quality focus**: Cheapest isn't always best

#### Nigerian Vendor Pricing Guide
| Service | Typical Range (₦) | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| Plumber (per visit) | 3,000-15,000 | + parts |
| Electrician (per visit) | 3,000-20,000 | + parts |
| AC servicing | 5,000-15,000 | Per unit |
| Generator service | 15,000-50,000 | Depends on size |
| Cleaning (monthly) | 30,000-100,000 | Per estate |
| Security (monthly) | 50,000-150,000 | Per estate |

### 4. Contract Management

#### Key Contract Terms
- **Scope**: Clear, detailed deliverables
- **Timeline**: Start, milestones, completion
- **Price**: Fixed, milestone-based, or T&M
- **Payment terms**: When and how paid
- **Warranty**: Period and coverage
- **Termination**: Conditions for ending
- **Dispute resolution**: How conflicts handled

#### Contract Review Checklist
- [ ] Scope clearly defined
- [ ] Price and payment terms clear
- [ ] Timeline realistic and agreed
- [ ] Warranty period adequate
- [ ] Insurance coverage verified
- [ ] References checked
- [ ] Termination clause fair
- [ ] Dispute resolution specified

## Nigerian Procurement Context

### Vendor Ecosystem
- **Informal sector**: Many sole proprietors
- **Payment terms**: Often cash-on-completion
- **Availability**: Weekday vs. weekend rates differ
- **Quality variance**: High — need vetting process
- **Relationships**: Personal connections matter

### Common Challenges
- **Quality inconsistency**: Same vendor, different quality
- **Price fluctuations**: Costs change frequently
- **Availability**: Good vendors are busy
- **Communication**: Many don't use email
- **Documentation**: Receipts/invoices not always provided

### Best Practices
1. **Build relationships**: With reliable vendors
2. **Get everything in writing**: Even informal agreements
3. **Verify references**: Don't just trust claims
4. **Start small**: Test before committing
5. **Pay fairly**: Build loyalty, get better service

### Vendor Communication Tips
- **WhatsApp**: Primary channel for most vendors
- **Voice notes**: Clearer than text for complex instructions
- **Photos**: Show the issue clearly
- **Follow up**: Don't assume they'll call back
- **Document**: Keep records of agreements
