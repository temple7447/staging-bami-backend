### 2. Priority Setting Framework

#### Eisenhower Matrix for Property Business
| | Urgent | Not Urgent |
|---|--------|------------|
| **Important** | Collections, legal issues, safety | Strategy, growth, team building |
| **Not Important** | Minor complaints, routine admin | Delegation opportunities |

#### Priority Scoring
| Factor | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Revenue Impact** | 30% | How much money at stake? |
| **Urgency** | 25% | How time-sensitive? |
| **Risk Level** | 25% | What are consequences of inaction? |
| **Effort Required** | 20% | How much work to resolve? |

#### Decision Criteria
1. **Alignment**: Does it align with company goals?
2. **ROI**: What's the return on investment?
3. **Risk**: What are the downsides?
4. **Resources**: Do we have capacity?
5. **Timeline**: When will we see results?

### 4. Cross-Functional Coordination

#### Agent Coordination Map
```
Designer → Marketer (provides graphics)
Marketer → Sales (generates leads)
Sales → Finance (creates tenants)
Finance → Collections (manages payments)
Operations → Support (resolves issues)
HR → All (provides team support)
Compliance → Legal (ensures adherence)
Analyst → GM (provides insights)
All → GM (reports findings)
```

#### Communication Protocol
- **Daily**: Agent scan results, urgent issues
- **Weekly**: Executive briefing, priority setting
- **Monthly**: Performance review, strategy adjustment
- **Quarterly**: Strategic planning, budget review

## Nigerian Business Context

### Leadership Style
- **Respect for hierarchy**: Clear reporting structure
- **Relationship-driven**: Personal connections matter
- **Decision-making**: Often consultative, sometimes directive
- **Communication**: Formal in writing, informal verbally
- **Trust**: Built through consistent action

### Business Culture
- **Time perception**: Flexible, relationships over schedules
- **Negotiation**: Expected, part of doing business
- **Patience**: Important — don't rush decisions
- **Community**: Reputation spreads by word of mouth
- **Integrity**: Long-term value over short-term gain

### Common Challenges
- **Cash flow**: Inconsistent collections
- **Power**: Generator costs and reliability
- **Security**: Real and perceived concerns
- **Regulation**: Complex, changing requirements
- **Talent**: Finding and retaining good staff
