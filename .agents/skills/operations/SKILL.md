# Operations Skill

Expert Nigerian property operations manager specializing in maintenance coordination, vendor management, and facility optimization.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Issue Triage System

| Priority | Response Time | Resolution Time | Examples |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Critical** | 1 hour | 24 hours | Security breach, major leak, power outage |
| **High** | 4 hours | 48 hours | Plumbing failure, electrical issue, broken lock |
| **Medium** | 24 hours | 5 days | Minor repairs, cosmetic issues |
| **Low** | 48 hours | 14 days | Requests, improvements, non-urgent |

### 2. Issue Categories & Vendor Matching

| Category | Typical Issues | Vendor Type | Avg. Cost (₦) |
|----------|---------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Plumbing** | Leaks, blocked drains, toilet issues | Plumber | 5,000-25,000 |
| **Electrical** | Faulty wiring, tripped breakers, lights | Electrician | 5,000-30,000 |
| **Structural** | Cracks, paint, doors, windows | Mason/Carpenter | 10,000-50,000 |
| **Security** | Locks, gates, cameras, access | Security vendor | 5,000-100,000 |
| **Cleaning** | Common areas, waste disposal | Cleaning service | 3,000-15,000 |
| **General** | AC, appliances, furniture | Handyman | 5,000-20,000 |

### 3. Vendor Management Framework

#### Vendor Evaluation Criteria
- **Response Time**: How quickly do they arrive?
- **Quality**: First-time fix rate
- **Cost**: Competitive pricing
- **Reliability**: Do they show up when scheduled?
- **Communication**: Do they update on progress?

#### Vendor Scorecard
| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Excellent — reliable, quality, fair price |
| 4 | Good — minor issues, still recommended |
| 3 | Average — acceptable but room for improvement |
| 2 | Below average — consider replacing |
| 1 | Poor — replace immediately |

## Nigerian Operations Context

### Common Challenges
- **Power supply**: Generator maintenance is critical
- **Water supply**: Borehole/pump maintenance essential
- **Security**: Guard staffing, access control
- **Waste management**: Disposal coordination
- **Rainy season**: Flooding, leaks, drainage issues

### Vendor Ecosystem
- **Informal sector**: Many vendors are sole proprietors
- **Payment terms**: Often cash-on-completion
- **Availability**: Weekday vs. weekend rates differ
- **Quality variance**: High — need vetting process

### Cost Optimization
- **Bulk contracts**: Negotiate fixed rates with reliable vendors
- **Preventive vs. Reactive**: Preventive is 3-5x cheaper
- **In-house capability**: Train staff for minor repairs
- **Spare parts inventory**: Stock common items
