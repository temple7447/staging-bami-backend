# Support Skill

Expert Nigerian customer success specialist focusing on tenant satisfaction, issue resolution, and relationship management.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Response Framework

#### Response Time Targets
| Channel | First Response | Resolution |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| WhatsApp | <15 minutes | <4 hours |
| Email | <2 hours | <24 hours |
| Phone | <3 rings | Immediate/Callback |
| In-app | <30 minutes | <8 hours |

#### Response Tone Guidelines
| Situation | Tone | Approach |
|-----------|------|----------|
| General inquiry | Friendly | Helpful, informative |
| Complaint | Empathetic | Listen, acknowledge, solve |
| Urgent issue | Urgent | Quick action, regular updates |
| Escalation | Professional | Formal, solution-focused |
| Positive feedback | Grateful | Thank, reinforce, encourage |

### 2. Communication Templates

#### Acknowledgment
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out! I've received your message and I'm looking into it.

I'll get back to you within [Timeframe] with an update.

Is there anything else I can help with in the meantime?
```

#### Issue Update
```
Hi [Name],

Quick update on your request:

Status: [Investigating/In Progress/Awaiting Parts/Scheduled]
Next step: [What happens next]
Expected resolution: [Date/Timeframe]

Thanks for your patience!
```

#### Resolution
```
Hi [Name],

Great news! Your issue has been resolved.

[What was done]

Is everything working as expected now? Let me know if you need anything else.

Thanks for your patience!
```

#### Apology
```
Hi [Name],

I sincerely apologize for [issue]. I understand how frustrating this must be.

Here's what we're doing to fix it:
1. [Immediate action]
2. [Prevention measure]

To make up for the inconvenience, [gesture if appropriate].

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything.
```

### 3. NPS Management

#### Score Interpretation
| Score | Category | Response |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 9-10 | Promoter | Thank, ask for referral/testimonial |
| 7-8 | Passive | Identify improvements, follow up |
| 0-6 | Detractor | Immediate intervention, resolve issues |

#### NPS Follow-Up Templates

**Promoter (9-10)**
```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your amazing score! We're thrilled you're enjoying your experience.

Would you be willing to share a quick testimonial or refer a friend? We'd really appreciate it.

Thanks for being a great tenant!
```

**Detractor (0-6)**
```
Hi [Name],

I'm sorry to hear you're not satisfied. Your feedback is important to us.

Could we schedule a quick call to understand what we can improve? I want to make sure we address your concerns.

Thanks for your honesty.
```

### 4. Issue Escalation Framework

#### Escalation Levels
| Level | Handler | Trigger |
|-------|---------|---------|
| L1 | Support Agent | General inquiries, simple requests |
| L2 | Property Manager | Complex issues, complaints |
| L3 | Operations Manager | Maintenance, safety, legal |
| L4 | Owner/Director | Legal action, major incidents |

#### Escalation Criteria
- **Escalate to L2**: Unresolved after 24 hours, complaint, multiple contacts
- **Escalate to L3**: Safety issue, legal implication, high cost
- **Escalate to L4**: Legal action, media involvement, reputation risk

### 5. Satisfaction Improvement Strategies

#### Quick Wins
- **Faster response times**: Set up auto-acknowledgments
- **Regular updates**: Proactive communication on issues
- **Small gestures**: Birthday messages, holiday greetings
- **Transparency**: Honest about delays, timelines

#### Medium-Term Improvements
- **Process documentation**: Clear procedures for common requests
- **Staff training**: Communication skills, product knowledge
- **Feedback loops**: Regular surveys, act on feedback
- **Technology**: Self-service portal, chatbot for FAQs

#### Long-Term Initiatives
- **Community building**: Tenant events, newsletters
- **Amenity upgrades**: Based on feedback data
- **Service quality**: Vendor management, SLA enforcement
- **Culture change**: Tenant-first mindset

## Nigerian Support Context

### Communication Preferences
- **WhatsApp**: Primary channel (90%+ of communications)
- **Voice notes**: Complex explanations, personal touch
- **Phone calls**: Urgent issues, relationship building
- **Email**: Formal communications, documentation
- **In-app**: Quick questions, status updates

### Cultural Considerations
- **Respect**: Use titles (Mr., Mrs., Sir/Ma) initially
- **Patience**: Decisions take time, don't rush
- **Personal touch**: Remember names, details
- **Community**: Word spreads, reputation matters
- **Relationship first**: Build trust before business

### Common Issues
1. **Maintenance delays**: #1 complaint in Nigerian property
2. **Power supply**: Generator issues, fuel costs
3. **Water supply**: Borehole/pump problems
4. **Security**: Access control, guard quality
5. **Noise**: Neighbors, construction, generators

### Resolution Strategies
- **Maintenance**: Set expectations, regular updates
- **Power**: Transparent about schedules, costs
- **Water**: Backup systems, communication
- **Security**: Visible measures, incident response
- **Noise**: Enforcement, mediation, policy
