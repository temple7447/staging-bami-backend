### 2. Agreement Expiry Tracker

#### Risk Levels
| Status | Risk Level | Action |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Active (>30 days) | ✅ Low | Monitor |
| Expiring soon (15-30 days) | ⚠️ Medium | Initiate renewal |
| Expiring soon (1-14 days) | 🔴 High | Urgent renewal |
| Expired (1-30 days) | 🔴 Critical | Regularize immediately |
| Expired (>30 days) | 🔴 Critical | Legal action required |

#### Renewal Checklist
- [ ] Send renewal notice (60 days before expiry)
- [ ] Negotiate terms (rent increase, conditions)
- [ ] Draft new agreement
- [ ] Both parties sign
- [ ] Collect stamp duty
- [ ] File signed agreement
- [ ] Update system records
- [ ] Issue tenancy receipt

### 3. Regulatory Compliance Framework

#### Nigerian Tenancy Laws
| Law | Applicability | Key Requirements |
|-----|---------------|------------------|
| Tenancy Law of Lagos State 2011 | Lagos | Written agreements, notice periods |
| Rent Tribunal (Lagos) | Lagos | Dispute resolution mechanism |
| Stamp Duties Act | All states | Stamp duty on agreements |
| Land Use Act | All states | Land tenure requirements |
| State-specific laws | Varies | Check local regulations |

#### Compliance Requirements
1. **Written Agreements**: All tenancies >3 years must be written
2. **Stamp Duty**: 3-6% of total rent (varies by state)
3. **Registration**: Some states require registration
4. **Notice Periods**: Must comply with law (not just agreement)
5. **Eviction Process**: Court order required (no self-help)

## Nigerian Compliance Context

### Common Compliance Gaps
1. **Expired agreements**: Tenants on informal holdover
2. **Missing documents**: Incomplete tenant files
3. **Unsigned addendums**: Verbal agreements not documented
4. **Missing receipts**: Cash payments not recorded
5. **Unstamped agreements**: Stamp duty not paid

### Risk Mitigation
- **Regular audits**: Monthly file checks
- **Automated alerts**: 60/30/15 day expiry warnings
- **Digital records**: Cloud-based document storage
- **Training**: Staff understanding of requirements
- **Legal counsel**: Relationship with property lawyer

### Legal Consequences of Non-Compliance
- **Eviction difficulty**: Cannot evict without proper process
- **Dispute weakness**: Weak position in tribunal
- **Tax penalties**: Unstamped agreements attract fines
- **Insurance void**: Non-compliance may void coverage
- **Reputation risk**: Poor practices damage brand
