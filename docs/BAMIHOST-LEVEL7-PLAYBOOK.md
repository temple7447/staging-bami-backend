# BamiHost — The Level 7 Action Playbook

Running **Bami Host** (and the property businesses on it) through the **7 Levels of Scale**
+ the **Scalable Operating System** (Ryan Deiss / Scalable.co framework).

Two lenses run in parallel:
- **🏢 Bami Host the platform/company** (the SaaS you're building)
- **🏠 The property owner** (your user, managing estates on Bami Host)

For each level: the goal, the action checklist, and **what Bami Host already automates**.
Tick the boxes in order — you cannot skip a level.

---

## ✅ Level 0 — Define Your Number (do this first)
- [ ] Write your **Number**: 3-yr revenue, profit, and valuation target
- [ ] Codify your **Why** (Me / Us / Them)
- [ ] Pick the goal type: **Double in Three** (26% CAGR) or **Top to Bottom**
- [ ] Fill the Scalable Impact Planner (start point → end game → why → how/Focus Five)
> 🤖 *Bami Host:* the **AI Coach** can walk an owner through this from their live numbers.

---

## 🥇 Level 1 — Sell & Serve 10
**Goal:** prove demand + delivery. 10 customers who score 9–10 NPS.

**🏠 Owner checklist**
- [ ] Fill 10 units / sign 10 tenants (real, paying)
- [ ] After each, run the **1-question NPS** ("0–10, would you recommend us?")
- [ ] Build the **Model 10 list** — best tenants (renew, pay on time, refer)

**🏢 Bami Host checklist**
- [ ] Onboard 10 paying property owners who actually run their estates on it
- [ ] NPS each; reach 10 promoters before scaling spend
> 🤖 *Already built:* tenant management, enquiries→lead scoring, payments. **Add:** an automated NPS ask after move-in / first payment (Finance or Sales agent).

---

## 🥈 Level 2 — Build the Growth Flywheel
**Goal:** ONE growth engine → ₦/$ equivalent of $10k/mo for 3 straight months.

**Checklist**
- [ ] Map your single biggest **Growth Engine** (how an owner/tenant actually arrives) — sticky-note flow
- [ ] Build a **Growth Scorecard** (metric per stage)
- [ ] Find the **bottleneck** stage
- [ ] Run **3 projects per quarter** against it (90-day flywheel: Map→Measure→Identify→Optimize)
- [ ] Don't launch a 2nd engine until "One, 10, Three" is hit
> 🤖 *Already built:* **Marketer agent** auto-creates listing posts + **Designer agent** graphics for vacant units; lead scoring on enquiries. **Add:** a growth scorecard view in the AI Agents hub.

---

## 🥉 Level 3 — Upgrade Your Operating System (the big one)
**Goal:** cross the "scalable line" — the business runs without the founder.

**Build the 3 components:**

**① Algorithms**
- [ ] Map the **Value Engines**: Growth, **Fulfillment** (rent collection, maintenance, tenant service), Innovation
- [ ] Identify **Power Stages** (2–3 critical tasks per engine)
- [ ] Write **Playbooks** for power stages (3 Ds: Define→Design→Deploy; blind-follow + field test)
- [ ] **High Output Team Canvas** — assign CABs (3–5 per person)

**② Common Language**
- [ ] **Scorecard**: 3 evergreen (revenue, profit, cash) + 3 North Star + 3 per team
- [ ] **Meeting rhythm**: quarterly offsite → monthly review → weekly check-in

**③ Desired Outputs**
- [ ] **Clarity Compass**: 3-yr target, Purpose (Contribution+Impact), 5–7 differentiated Core Values, Strategic Anchors
> 🤖 *This is what Bami Host IS.* Each **AI agent = an automated power stage**: Designer, Marketer, Sales, Finance, Operations, HR. The **Business Health score** + Autopilot = the scorecard. **Add:** a Clarity Compass section + value-engine map in-app.

---

## 🏅 Level 4 — Double Your Take-Home (profit, not growth)
**Goal:** consistent personal income; *growth eats profit*.

**Checklist (4 cash principles)**
- [ ] **Pay Yourself First** — salary = living expenses + 15%
- [ ] **Expense Ratios** — Revenue − Profit = Expenses (decide profit first); target 10–20% / Rule of 40
- [ ] **Emergency Fund** — 1-month operating reserve → build to 3–6 months
- [ ] **Cash Sweep** — monthly, mid-month after rent/payroll
> 🤖 *Already built:* **Finance agent** (overdue reminders, payment links), AI forecast, cashflow. **Add:** owner pay-yourself-first + expense-ratio tracker; auto cash-sweep flagging.

---

## 🎖️ Level 5 — Build Your Advisory Board
- [ ] Recruit 1 **mentor** (done what you're doing) + 3–5 **peers** (4–7:1 peers:mentor)
- [ ] Add **strategics** (lawyer, etc.) for seasons; **influencers** for credibility
- [ ] Pay mentors ($5k–$20k/yr) or reciprocate peer seats
- [ ] Run the **4-point meeting**: numbers, highlights, lowlights, where you need help (≥monthly)
> 🤖 *Add:* the **AI Coach as your always-on advisor** between board meetings; a board-meeting prep pack from live data.

---

## 🏆 Level 6 — Expand Through Acquisition
- [ ] Adopt the **investor identity** (update profiles, tell your network)
- [ ] Build a **deal team** (M&A specialist, transaction attorney, strategics)
- [ ] Define **acquisition criteria** (which of 5 deal types: channel, talent, market share, margin, enterprise value)
- [ ] Identify **5 targets**, do outreach, close + **integrate** (plan integration before closing)
> 🏠 *Owner play:* acquire another landlord's portfolio / a managing agent. 🏢 *Bami Host play:* acquire a competing prop-tech list or agency.

---

## 👑 Level 7 — Hit Your Number
- [ ] Confirm you hit the Number (revenue/profit/valuation)
- [ ] Claim your pre-planned **Everest reward**
- [ ] Choose your **second mountain** (scale again / new venture / non-business)
- [ ] Pick your **exit** (line → staff → org chart → board → ownership) — plan in advance
- [ ] Consider the **entrepreneurial investor** path; mentor someone one level below

---

## How to use this
1. **Diagnose your level** = the last level where you've ticked *every* box.
2. Focus **only** on that level's goal — don't jump ahead.
3. Ask the **AI Coach** ("/coach" on Telegram or the web widget) — it now teaches this exact framework from your live numbers.
