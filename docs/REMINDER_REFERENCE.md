# Rent Reminder System - Quick Reference Card\n\n## 📋 What It Does\nAutomatically sends email reminders to **tenants** and **admins** when rent is due:\n- **7 days before** due date → Email reminder\n- **3 days before** due date → Email reminder\n- **1 day before** due date → Email reminder\n\n**Runs every day at 08:00 AM automatically.**\n\n---\n\n## 🎯 Key Requirements\n\n✅ Tenant must have:\n- `tenantEmail` (required for emails)\n- `nextDueDate` (when rent is due)\n- `status` = 'occupied' OR 'pending'\n\n✅ Admin must have:\n- `isActive: true`\n- `role` = 'admin' OR 'super_admin'\n- Email configured\n\n---\n\n## 📁 New Files\n\n```\nmodels/ReminderLog.js ............. Tracks sent reminders\nutils/reminderService.js .......... Core reminder logic\nutils/scheduler.js ................ Cron job scheduler\n```\n\n## 🔧 Modified Files\n\n```\nutils/emailService.js ............ Added 2 email functions\nserver.js ........................ Initialize scheduler\n```\n\n---\n\n## ⚙️ Configuration\n\n### Set Email in
